About Me

Radhika Malpani is a photographer drawn to portrait and tableau-style imagery, inspired by everyday life and interested in capturing the beauty in fleeting, often unnoticed scenes.

Her work explores the intersection of culture, architecture, and human moments across continents, from the serene winter landscapes of Fairbanks to the vibrant streets of Hong Kong, and the architectural grandeur of Jaipur.

Based between Tokyo, Mumbai and New York
